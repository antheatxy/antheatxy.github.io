AI Chat - Training Data
Hi!! Can you explain how training data is used in AI? How does AI learn from it?
Great question! AI uses training data to identify patterns and make predictions. The more accurate and diverse the data, the better the AI’s learning.
What kind of data does AI use to learn?
AI typically uses large sets of labeled data, such as text, images, or sound. The data is often labeled with the correct answers so that the AI can 'learn' the relationships between input and output.
Can AI get better with more data?
Of course! More diverse data leads to better model generalization, meaning it can make more accurate predictions on new, unseen data.
Where does the training data come from?
Training data for AI comes from many places, like books, websites, images, surveys, or anything else that involves human-generated content!
